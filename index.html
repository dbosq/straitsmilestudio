About The Doctors

Michele Strait

Born in Colorado, Dr. Michele Strait moved closer to her family roots in Michigan at the age of twelve. She spent half a decade in orthodontic treatment with headgear and braces - which may explain her passion for good alignment but in a much more comfortable way. She attended the the University of Michigan undergraduate and School of Dentistry, graduating with honors.

Following graduation, she began her career surrounding herself with mentors. Practicing as Michele Platson, she worked in a private dental offices in Chicago and then Austin with multiple dentists and dental specialists. Over the course of ten years, she practiced adjacent to many dental professionals with a range of philosophies and refined her own perspective.

During her time in Texas, she has been captivated by food, wine, music, art, and culture. Michele has made the Austin area her home for the past 7 years and soaks up time in the warm weather with her fianc√© and labrador. As a resident of Brentwood she loves visiting the local restaurants and shops on Burnet Road.

About The Doctors

John Babineau

Born in Boston, MA and raised in Claremont New Hampshire, he attended the University of New Hampshire, where he was a member of Signa Nu fraternity. After graduating from UNH with a B.S. in Biochemistry, Dr. Babineau had the opportunity to move to Boston to attend dental school, and there he met his future wife, Dr. Lori Babineau.

He graduated with his DMD degree from Tufts University School of Dental Medicine, and had an opportunity to practice in the private practice of a faculty member in the Restorative Department at Tufts University School of Dental Medicine. This experience in working closely with an highly experienced clinician provided Dr. Babineau with the foundation to treat challenging and complex dental cases.

While the experience of living in Boston was exhilarating both academically and culturally, Dr. Babineau was anxious to move to Texas, where his wife was born and raised. Dr. John Babineau and his wife, Dr. Lori Babineau established their practice in Austin in the early nineties and have practiced together until 2018. Dr. John Babineau has another dental business that helps the Military Reserves and National Guard maintain their dental readiness.
